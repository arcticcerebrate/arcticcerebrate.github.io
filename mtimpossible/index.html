You are a climbing probe. Your goal is to reach the yellow beacon at the end of all five levels.
Construct walls(pylons) to block and protect your climber(probe) from boulders(ultralisks) as you climb.
Revive fallen teammates by bringing their tombstone(flag) to the safe zone located at the beginning of each level.
Two out of four randomly chosen shop items are available to assist you.
