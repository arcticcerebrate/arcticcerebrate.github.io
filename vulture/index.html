Command a single vulture against an onslaught of enemies.
Your greatest asset is not the vehicle plating of your bike,
nor your pack full of trusty fragmentation grenades,
nor the Ion Thrusters which make your bike the fastest in the Starcraft Universe.
Instead, your greatest asset is your skill in Vulture Micro.

Defy the odds along with other players against the computer which dares to challenge your Vulture Micro prowess.
You are one Vulture against The World.
Features:
- 5 Warm-Up Levels for those new to Vulture Micro. If the entire team chooses Veteran-Status, these first 5 are skipped, so the team can face off against tougher units at the start.
- Total of 18 levels, enough gameplay and enemies to satisfy players of varying skill level.
- Training Grounds: Even if you are struck down, you can continue to fine-tune your skills at the training grounds while the rest of the team battles to revive you.
- AFK-Mode keeps you safe when you need to leave the computer without interrupting the other players. It then allows you to continue battle after you have returned.
- [Boss] Heroes, with their high hit-points, test your ability in prolonged one-on-one vulture micro.
- [Rage] Heroes are smarter than regular Bosses. Rage-Bosses will cut corners to quickly close the distance between you and itself.
- [Hyper] Heroes are even smarter than Rage-Bosses. Hyper-Bosses predict your vulture's trajectory and attempt to intercept you.
- [Micro] Crackling. Only the best ever get a chance to face off against this boss unit. It is the only Boss that performs its own Micro against yours in a deadly game of chance and reflexes.
